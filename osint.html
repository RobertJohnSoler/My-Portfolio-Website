Combining OSINT with AI

This project combines 3 intelligences: Artificial Intelligence, Open-source Intelligence, and Threat Intelligence. In this project, I made a tool that asks for your target individual's social media URLs, and then gives you a personal, profesional, social, and psychological report on him/her from a threat intel standpoint.

This tool can give your target's employment history, educational background, recent locations, family members, likes and dislikes, contact information, and even PII.
