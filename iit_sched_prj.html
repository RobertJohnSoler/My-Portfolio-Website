Making students' lives easier with our course scheduling assistant app

This is a web application that my team made born out of our frustrations in creating good schedules for our semesters. It recommends course schedules for the next semester given the student's major and completed courses. It also saves this information so that the student can keep using the app again and again over different semesters.

I was the full-stack developer in this team. I was in charge of doing a good portion of the website and most of the API running in the back-end. I was also in charge of application security, and that includes database security and secure logins to user accounts.
