Tricking, escaping, and frustrating forensic investigators

In this paper, I discuss the many ways cybercriminals can evade security analysts and digital forensic investigators. It contains anti-forensic techniques for Windows and iOS machines, which are the top 2 and top 3 most popular operating systems today.

I also find it rather ironic that this was originally written as a final paper for my digital forensics class. My forensics professor ended up liking a paper about the ways we can beat people like him.
