Keeping computers safe with our very own EDR solution

In this project, I worked with a team to develop an Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) app called CyberGoose. It continuously monitors a host machine for vulnerabilities and threats, and deals with them automatically. It then sends data to our server, which aggregates the data and shows my team security details and statistics of all the computers being monitored.
